Welcome

Georginio Wijnaldum arrived in style at AS Roma this week. Having endured a miserable season with Paris Saint-Germain on the back of his Liverpool exit, the Dutch midfielder has decided to jump onboard the exciting project that Jose Mourinho is curating at the Stadio Olimpico. On the back of securing Roma’s first piece of silverware in 14 years in the form of the UEFA Europa Conference League, the ‘Special One’ is now building a squad that’s more than capable of competing for Serie A glory.
